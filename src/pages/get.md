# get a copy!

there's different ways to get a magazine copy, depending on whether you're a teen or an adult.

### for teenagers

> **if you submitted a project to magazine:**
>
> you might receive one for free, or be able to get one at a discount! details aren't confirmed yet, so wait a little longer :)

you can buy one from the [stardance](https://stardance.hackclub.com/) shop! stardance is an event by hack club where you make projects, earn stardust, and buy cool prizes (like this magazine) from the shop.

it costs 🌟 **100 stardust** - that's around 10 hours of coding. once you've finished + shipped your project, you can buy it using stardust, and it's completely free 😼

[https://stardance.hackclub.com/shop/category/swag](https://stardance.hackclub.com/shop/category/swag)

it's also available from some other events too!

### for adults

you can buy a physical copy and we'll mail it straight to you! it costs **$12.81 USD** per copy in the USA, or **$20.18 USD including shipping** if you're outside the USA. 

[forms.hackclub.com/magazine-shop](https://forms.hackclub.com/magazine-shop)
